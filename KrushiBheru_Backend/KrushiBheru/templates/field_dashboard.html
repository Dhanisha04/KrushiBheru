Field NDVI/Pest Mapping

Green Area

The green area on the map represents healthy vegetation with good crop vigor, optimal soil moisture, and low stress from pests or diseases. Maintaining and expanding these areas can improve crop yield and sustainability.

Red Area

The red area indicates stressed or unhealthy crops, possibly due to water scarcity, pest infestation, soil nutrient deficiency, or disease. These areas require attention such as improved irrigation, pest control, or fertilization to recover crop health.

Warning

If an area is marked red on the map, it signals a potential risk to crop health and yield. Immediate investigation and corrective measures should be undertaken to prevent further deterioration.
